Diploma in practical electronics

This diploma contains three very special courses... with content designed to transfer anyone who does not have any electronic background to professionalism in electronic design based on abstract electronics without programming... The courses begin by explaining the basics and delve into the work of each element separately, and then begin with your foundation for building interactive circuits. - Smart - Specialized... It consists of 100 videos for the basics of practical electronics, 35 videos for digital electronics, and 75 videos for professional electronic design, and ends with the design of various printed circuits using copper or aluminium...

Through it, you will learn about how we produce simple and cheap products like China and how engineers of major companies think about designing electronic systems...

Number of hours: 100 hours of recorded video, divided into

210 videos, 300 hours, with practical application

Practical electronics course

First level: Introduction to electricity and simple electronic components

Implement simple circuits using basic components to perform specific functions

second level: Dealing with semiconductors of all kinds

Practical implementation of simple circuits using transistors, diodes, thyristors and triacs

third level: The third level: dealing with basic integrated circuits professionally

Implementing circuits on the 555 timer, amplifiers and filters

Digital electronics course

First level: Introduction to the world of digital, the digital system, and logic gates

Implement innovative simple circuits on each gate

second level: Building simple digital systems using popular design methods

Dealing with Karnoff's map and using it to develop systems

third level: Dealing with combination sequential circuits to implement difficult tasks

Implementing circuits on flip-flops, registers and counters

fourth level: Introduction to the types of signals, conversion between them, and modern systems

Introduction to microcontroller systems and their installation

Professional electronic design course

First level: Simple electronic design

Implement simple circuits using basic components to perform specific functions

second level: Advanced electronic design

Implement simple circuits using basic components to perform decision-making or control functions

third level: Special box circuits

Getting to know very famous special circuits enables you to understand the structure of large complex circuits

fourth level: Interactive electronic design

Implementing simple circuits using well-known integrated circuits to create interactive and intelligent functions

Fifth level: Professional electronic design

Implementing simple circuits using special integrated circuits to implement specialized tasks

Level Six: Printed Board (PCB) Design

Using software, implementing electronic diagrams and manufacturing them for commercial use

Who is the course designed for?

Everyone loves learning...an electronics hobbyist, a school or university student, or professional ... The course can reach the lowest levels and advance you to a level comparable to a professional design engineer...

What will you achieve in the course?

You will achieve solid knowledge of the basics of electronic design... and you will not need any other reference after that

You will develop the skills of working circuits practically... and recognize most of the components

You will achieve a level that qualifies you to move to another level to enter the world of electronic design and programmed controllers

What will you get?

You will get a collection of high-quality videos that show you practical business applications using basic electronic components

You will get circuit diagrams on most of the ideas and practical implementations narrated in the video

Practical electronics course

A course in Arabic consisting of 100 videos in which the principles of electronics are explained from scratch to professionalism... and the practical aspect is what this course requires... and after that anyone who is interested in electronics and wants to continue.

Digital electronics course

An Arabic course consisting of 33 videos explaining in a practical way the principles of digital electronics from scratch and its most important circuits to put you on the first step to the digital world and dealing with logic cells, which are the basis of programmed controllers.

Professional electronic design

An Arabic language course consisting of 75 videos divided into 6 levels in electronic design... starting from simple design and ending with professional design and printed circuit design... all of this in a precise, practical manner and of high quality.
