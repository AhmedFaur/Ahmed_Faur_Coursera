Who is the course designed for?

Everyone loves learning...an electronics hobbyist, a school or university student, or professional ... The course can reach the lowest levels and advance you to a level comparable to a professional design engineer...

What will you achieve in the course?

You will achieve solid knowledge of the basics of electronic design... and you will not need any other reference after that

You will develop the skills of working circuits practically... and recognize most of the components

You will achieve a level that qualifies you to move to another level to enter the world of electronic design and programmed controllers

What will you get?

You will get a collection of high-quality videos that show you practical business applications using basic electronic components

You will get circuit diagrams on most of the ideas and practical implementations narrated in the video
